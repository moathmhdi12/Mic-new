About us

Welcome to MIC NSW, your trusted partner in Sydney for exceptional home and commercial renovations. With a passion for transforming spaces, we specialize in breathing new life into properties through innovative design and superior craftsmanship. From modern kitchen and bathroom makeovers to complete home overhauls, we are dedicated to creating spaces that are not only beautiful but also highly functional and tailored to your lifestyle. Our commitment to excellence, clear communication, and meticulous attention to detail ensures a seamless and stress-free renovation experience from start to finish.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to be the leading renovation company in Sydney, known for our integrity, creativity, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We aim to set the standard for excellence in the renovation industry, creating lasting value for our clients and our community.

  • Our Mision

    Our mission is to transform spaces and elevate lifestyles by delivering top-quality renovation services. We are committed to exceeding our clients' expectations through innovative design, exceptional craftsmanship, and a professional, transparent process. We strive to create environments that our clients will love for years to come.

Our Process

Consultation & Design

We start with a free, no-obligation consultation to discuss your vision, budget, and project goals. We then work with you to create a custom design that meets your needs

Quotations & Plannings

We provide a detailed and transparent quote with a clear breakdown of costs and a project timeline. Once approved, we handle all necessary planning and permits

Construction

Our professional team gets to work, maintaining a tidy and safe workspace while bringing your design to life with precision and care

Completion & Handover

We conduct a final walk-through with you to ensure every detail is perfect. We don't consider the job complete until you are 100% satisfied with your new space
